About MentorConnect

Empowering grassroots and student startups by connecting them with the right mentors and resources.

Our Vision

To support aspiring entrepreneurs from underserved regions and campuses by bridging the knowledge gap through mentoring and community engagement.

Our Mission

We aim to build a trusted platform where experienced mentors can share insights with the next generation of innovators and guide them through their startup journey.

Why This Matters

Startups at the grassroots level often lack access to expert advice. MentorConnect ensures that no startup is left behind due to lack of guidance or resources.
